The Problem Every Nigerian School Faces

Nigerian parents care deeply about their children's safety. Yet every school day, they face the same unanswered questions:

❓ Did my child arrive at school safely this morning?

❓ Who picked up my child after school today?

❓ Was my child actually present at school today?

❓ What if an unauthorized person tried to take my child?

Manual school registers and sign-in sheets provide no real-time information. Parents cannot verify attendance. Schools have limited ability to confirm authorized pickups. When incidents occur — delayed notification is the norm, not the exception.

In today's competitive private school environment, the schools that earn lasting parent loyalty are those that take student safety seriously — and communicate it visibly and consistently.

The SafeTrack Solution

SafeTrack works simply. Each student carries an ID card with a unique code. When gate staff scan the card at the school gate — arrival or departure — the system takes over automatically.

📲

Instant SMS to Parent

The moment a student is scanned, their parent receives an SMS with the student's name, school name and exact time. Works on all Nigerian networks — MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9mobile.

📊

Automatic Attendance Records

Every scan is permanently saved. School admin can view today's attendance in real time or check records for any previous date — at any time.

🔐

Secure Pickup Authorization

When a parent sends someone else to collect their child, they generate a time-limited 6-digit PIN. Gate staff verify the PIN and the person's identity before releasing the child.

📱

Parent Visibility Platform

Parents access a simple app to view their child's attendance history, check registration status and generate pickup authorizations — anytime, from any phone.

Safety & Pickup Verification

SafeTrack's pickup verification system ensures no child leaves your school with an unauthorized person — through three independent layers of protection that work simultaneously.

🪪

Student ID Verification

Every student carries a unique ID card that cannot be shared or transferred. Lost cards are deactivated instantly from the system.

⏱️

Time-Limited Pickup PIN

Parents generate a single-use PIN valid for 30 minutes. Even if intercepted, it expires before it can be misused.

👤

Identity Confirmation

Gate staff ask the pickup person to state their name before checking the system. A mismatch means the child is not released — period.

⚠️ Critical staff instruction: Always ask the pickup person their name before showing them the screen. The name they say must match the name shown. If it does not match — the child is not released under any circumstances.
