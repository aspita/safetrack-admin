Executive Summary

SafeTrack is a school safety and attendance tracking system designed specifically for Nigerian private schools. It solves one of the most pressing concerns of Nigerian parents — not knowing if their child arrived safely at school or who picked them up.

Using QR code technology, SafeTrack automatically sends an SMS to parents the moment their child's ID card is scanned at the school gate — on arrival and on departure. Every single school day.

SafeTrack also includes a secure Pickup PIN system that ensures no child can ever be collected by an unauthorized person — giving schools and parents complete peace of mind.

⚠️

The Problem We Are Solving

Every day, thousands of Nigerian parents face the same anxiety — they send their children to school in the morning and spend the entire day wondering:

❓ Did my child arrive safely?
❓ Who picked up my child after school?
❓ Was my child at school today?
❓ What if an unauthorized person took my child?

Traditional school registers and manual sign-in sheets are unreliable, time consuming and give parents no real-time information. SafeTrack changes all of this with technology that works automatically — every single day.

✅

The SafeTrack Solution

SafeTrack gives every student a unique QR code on their ID card. When gate staff scan the card at the school gate, the system automatically:

📲

Sends SMS to Parent

Parent receives an instant text message with the student's name, school and exact time of arrival or departure.

📊

Logs Attendance

Every scan is permanently saved with date and time. School admin can view full attendance history for any date.

🔐

Secures Pickup

Parents generate a secure 6-digit PIN for authorized pickup persons. PIN expires in 30 minutes and is single use.

🎙️

Voice Greeting

The system says each student's name out loud when scanned — "Welcome to school Amaka! Have a super day!"

📱

What Parents Receive

The moment a student's ID card is scanned at the gate, their parent receives an SMS on their phone — instantly. Works on all Nigerian networks: MTN, Airtel, Glo and 9mobile.

How It Works — Step by Step

1

School signs up with SafeTrack

We set up your school on SafeTrack within 48 hours. Your school receives a private dashboard, unique login credentials and full access to the system.

2

Parents register their children

Parents open the SafeTrack parent app on their phone and register their child in less than 5 minutes. They enter the child's name, class, take a passport photo and provide their contact details. No paperwork. No queues.

3

SafeTrack prints and delivers ID cards

We print a professional plastic ID card for every registered student — with their passport photo and a unique QR code. Cards are delivered to the school within 48 hours of registration.

4

Gate staff scan ID cards every day

Gate staff use any regular smartphone to scan student ID cards at the gate — every morning on arrival and every afternoon on departure. The system beeps, announces the student's name and logs the time automatically.

5

Parents receive SMS instantly

Every parent receives an SMS the moment their child is scanned. Arrival in the morning. Departure in the afternoon. Every single school day — completely automatic.

🔒

Three Layers of Security

SafeTrack is the only school safety system in Nigeria with three independent security layers protecting every child simultaneously.

🪪

Layer 1 — QR Code

Unique to every student. Cannot be copied. Lost cards are deactivated instantly.

⏱️

Layer 2 — Time PIN

Pickup PINs expire in 30 minutes and can only be used once. Stolen PINs become useless fast.

👤

Layer 3 — Name Check

Staff ask pickup person's name before checking screen. Name must match — or child is not released.
