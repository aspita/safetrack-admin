The Problem Every Nigerian Parent Knows

Every morning, parents drop their children at school and spend the whole day wondering — Did my child arrive safely? Who picked them up? Is my child okay?

When something goes wrong — a child gets lost, picked up by the wrong person, or leaves school without permission — parents are the last to know.

💡 SafeTrack solves this. The moment your child walks through the school gate and their ID card is scanned — you receive an SMS on your phone. Instantly. Every single day.

Everything SafeTrack Does

One system. Everything your school needs to keep children safe.

🪪

Student ID Cards with QR Codes

Every student gets a professional plastic ID card with their photo and a unique QR code. SafeTrack prints and delivers the cards to your school. No name or personal info is displayed on the card — it is all encrypted in the QR code for maximum security.

📷

Instant QR Code Scanning

Gate staff use any smartphone to scan student ID cards as they arrive and depart. The system beeps, says the student's name out loud, and logs the exact time. No internet required — just a phone and a camera.

📲

Automatic SMS to Parents

The moment a student is scanned, their parent receives an SMS text message with the student's name, school name and exact time. Works on MTN, Airtel, Glo and 9mobile. No WhatsApp needed — just a regular phone number.

🔐

Secure Pickup PIN System

When a parent sends someone else to pick up their child, they generate a secure 6-digit PIN from the SafeTrack app. The PIN expires in 30 minutes and can only be used once. Gate staff verify the PIN and the pickup person's name before releasing the child.

📊

Full Attendance Records

Every arrival and departure is automatically saved with the date and time. School admin can view today's attendance or check any previous date. Parents can also view their child's complete attendance history from the parent app.

🎙️

Friendly Voice Greetings

When a student is scanned, the system says their name out loud in a warm, child-friendly voice — "Welcome to school Amaka! Have a super day!" Students love it. It makes the school gate experience positive every morning.

Parents register their children

🔒 Three Layers of Security

SafeTrack is the only school safety system in Nigeria with three independent security layers. Even if one layer fails — the other two still protect your child.

🪪

Layer 1 — QR Code

Every student has a unique QR code that cannot be copied or duplicated. If a card is lost, it is deactivated immediately from the system.

⏱️

Layer 2 — Time PIN

Pickup PINs expire after 30 minutes and can only be used once. Even if the PIN is stolen or shared — it becomes useless within minutes.

👤

Layer 3 — Name Check

Gate staff ask the pickup person to state their name before checking the system. If the name does not match — the child is not released. Simple human security.
