The Problem We Are Solving

Every day, thousands of Nigerian parents send their children to school without knowing exactly when they arrived or who picked them up. SafeTrack changes that — giving parents real-time peace of mind and schools a powerful security tool.

What SafeTrack Does

📷

QR Code Scanning

Each student has a unique QR code on their ID card. Staff scan it at the gate using any smartphone — no special equipment needed.

📱

Instant Parent Notifications

The moment a student is scanned, their parent receives an SMS and WhatsApp message with the exact time of arrival or departure.

🔐

Secure Pickup PIN

When someone else picks up a child, the parent generates a secure 6-digit PIN valid for 30 minutes. Staff verify the PIN and the pickup person's name before releasing the child.

How It Works

1

School Onboards

We register the school and all students. Each student gets a unique QR code ID card.

2

Student Scans at Gate

Staff scan the student's ID card QR code when they arrive or leave school.

3

Parent Gets Notified

Parent instantly receives SMS and WhatsApp message with the time and school name.

4

Pickup is Secured

Parent generates a PIN for the pickup person. Staff verify PIN and name before releasing child.
